Admissions Committee Member Confidentiality & Conflict of Interest Agreement

Kornberg School of Dentistry

Important
Admissions Committee members are entrusted with highly confidential applicant information and are expected to uphold the highest standards of professionalism, confidentiality, and ethical conduct.

An Admissions Committee member holds a special position of trust within the Kornberg School of Dentistry. Committee members have access to highly sensitive confidential material and participate in confidential discussions regarding candidates for admission. The fairness of the admissions process requires every committee member to adhere to the highest standards of ethical conduct and professionalism.

Included in these standards is the responsibility of Admissions Committee members to ensure and maintain absolute confidentiality of all aspects of the admissions process. This obligation includes, but is not limited to, refraining from any disclosure or discussion of applicant information or Admissions Committee deliberations with anyone not on the Admissions Committee or Admissions staff.

Failure to maintain absolute confidentiality is a serious breach of ethics and may be addressed through all appropriate disciplinary procedures of the Kornberg School of Dentistry and Temple University.

Admissions Committee members with a conflict of interest regarding a particular candidate must recuse themselves from voting or participating in discussions pertaining to that candidate. Conflicts of interest include, but are not limited to, family members, candidates with close personal or family relationships, and business associates or their family members.

The Kornberg School of Dentistry requires that members of the Admissions Committee acknowledge, through their signature and agreement, their responsibility to maintain absolute confidentiality and disclose any conflict of interest situations that may exist.

Statement of Admissions Committee Member

I acknowledge that as an Admissions Committee member I will be responsible for reviewing and discussing privileged and confidential information, and that I will have access to documents, data, and other information, all of which is highly confidential and may be discussed only with individuals directly involved in the admissions process.

Agreement

I agree that I shall never, directly or indirectly, disclose to any other person or entity any information related to the admissions process or any other privileged or confidential information acquired in the course of serving as an Admissions Committee member. Furthermore, I agree that I will recuse myself from voting and participating in discussions whenever a conflict of interest involving a candidate arises.

I have read and understood the confidentiality agreement herein. Furthermore, I have been given the opportunity to ask questions and have been provided with satisfactory answers to those questions.
